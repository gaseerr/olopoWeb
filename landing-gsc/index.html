Franchise
Model

The GSC system includes Warehouse Franchise, Cluster Franchises, Merchants, and Users. The Warehouse Franchise gathers products and aids Cluster Franchises. Cluster Franchises oversee local shops, work with users, and get supplies from the Warehouse Franchise. Merchants sell products to users using the system's network. Users can buy from Cluster Franchises or merchants and access a range of items.

What is

Warehouse
Franchise?

Warehouse franchises serve as the backbone, overseeing and bolstering cluster franchises to ensure top-tier products. Each warehouse manages up to 10 clusters, fostering streamlined operations. Their pivotal role extends to linking local farmers with consumers, enhancing the system's connectivity. Strategically positioned across the North, Central, and South zones of Kerala, our warehouses ensure optimal distribution by aligning with market needs, accessibility, and future growth prospects.

What is

Cluster
Franchise?

Our network includes cluster franchises working with post offices as local representatives. They bring in merchants to specific areas. These cluster franchises connect our main franchise with local communities. They sell various products from our warehouse and ensure fast delivery to customers, keeping the supply chain smooth.

Frequently Asked Questions

Olopo, is a digital platform integrating various services. It connects farmers, sellers, buyers, and more on a single platform.

A Warehouse Franchise acts as the system's backbone, overseeing clusters to ensure high-quality products. Each warehouse manages up to 10 clusters, linking farmers with consumers.

Warehouse Franchisees benefit from an established model, training, financial aid, and advanced software. They can potentially generate a monthly profit of 30 lakhs with a minimum investment.

Requirements to join as a Warehouse Franchisee include storage space, land, vehicles, employees, a deposit, and revenue assurance. The process involves appointments, evaluation, and setup.

Cluster Franchises represent the main franchise locally, ensuring smooth supply chain operations and fast customer delivery.

Cluster Franchisees gain ownership, market access, high ROI, and financial security through an ESCROW account and business loans.

Requirements for Cluster Franchisees include area, space, vehicles, employees, investment, and revenue assurance. The application process mirrors that of Warehouse Franchises.

The franchise application process involves appointments, evaluation, agreement signing, and operational setup for both Warehouse and Cluster Franchises.
