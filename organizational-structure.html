Holding Company

A holding company at the country level will oversee the organizational structure.

Subsidiary Companies

Under the umbrella of our holding company, we have established subsidiary companies dedicated to specific functions. These include HR subsidiaries, software subsidiaries, and hospitality subsidiaries, among others.

State Subsidiaries

Each subsidiary is further divided into state subsidiaries, each catering to the unique requirements of a specific geographical region.

Zone Warehouses

State subsidiaries manage and oversee three distinct zones, each equipped with its own network of warehouses to facilitate efficient operations.

Warehouse Franchises

Within each zone, our warehouse franchises play a vital role in managing and distributing our products and services.

Cluster Franchises

At the grassroots level, we have cluster franchises that operate with a focus on serving local communities. Each cluster franchise corresponds to a specific post office, ensuring personalized service and accessibility.

Our intricate organizational structure is designed to provide the highest level of service and efficiency to our valued customers and partners.
